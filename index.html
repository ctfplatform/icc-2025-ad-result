Attack & Defense

Welcome to the ICC 2025 Attack & Defense competition!

The International Cybersecurity Challenge (ICC) is a global CTF competition for youth cybersecurity talents. Attack & Defense (A&D) is a competition format where teams earn points by stealing Flags from other teams' vulnerable services while simultaneously keeping their own services running through bug fixes, monitoring, and defensive measures.
